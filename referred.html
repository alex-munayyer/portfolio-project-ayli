Earn More The More People Use Ayli

The more people sign up to get paid for their data, the more companies sign up to buy it. Similarly, the more companies that sign up to buy data, the more people sign up to earn money for it.

This cycle is known as a network effect. With each new person that uses Ayli, it enables you to earn more for your data. Also, it forces companies to start paying you directly whenever they use it.

Businesses currently make around $400 billion monetizing people's information every year. We think its time you get paid for it instead.
